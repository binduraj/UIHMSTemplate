Srinivas Gadikoppula
Trauma & Orthopaedics (MBBS, MS ( Ortho ) , FRCS (CSIG . FRCS)
Consultant Trauma & Orthopaedic Surgeon , North Middlesex University Hospital, London Associate Professor Trauma & Orthopaedics , St.George’s University , Grenada Undergraduate and Postgraduate Lead , Trauma & Orthopaedics ( UCL & RNOH). I have qualified from Osmania University , Hyderabad , India with MBBS in 1991, followed by MS in Trauma & Orthopaedics in 1994 from Gandhi Medical college, Secundrabad , India in 1994. After passing PLAB exam , London , I have done my basic surgical training at Black Notley and Colchester General Hospital , United Kingdom . I have been admitted as Fellow of Royal college of Physicians & Surgeons of Glasgow after passing FRCS ( Clinical surgery in General ) in 1998. My Specialist Training in Trauma & Orthopaedics has began at Royal National Orthopaedic ,Stanmore , London and I have completed FRCS in Trauma and Orthopaedics in 2005. After completing my Training I have been appointed as Consultant in Trauma & Orthopaedics at North Middlesex University Hospital in 2006 where I consult and deal with all General Orthopaedics and Trauma conditions including Hip and knee surgery with special interest in Upper limb conditions. Teaching comes natural to me. I am involved in Training Medical students from University college of London ( UCL ) and St. George’s ,Grenada. I am also involved in Training Post Graduate students from UCL and Royal National Orthopaedic Hospital, London. My extensive Training both in India and United kingdom has helped me to be a Good doctor and surgeon. I am glad to share my experience of Diagnosing my patients conditions accurately, involving them in their management and aiming to achieve Excellent Surgical Outcomes as expected by patients with minimal or NO complications . My outcomes of surgical procedures are available - National Joint Registry ( NJR ) www.njrcentre.org.uk Patient Feedback - I want great care (www.iwantgreatcare.org.uk) Mr Srinivas Gadikoppula was awarded the iWantGreatCare certificate of excellence in 2020 for delivering outstanding care.
